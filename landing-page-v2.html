Questions We Get Asked

"Is this actually ChatGPT managing my ads, or is that just marketing?"

It's real. ChatGPT (and Claude, and other AI tools) can connect to external systems through a standard called MCP — Model Context Protocol. We build the bridge between your AI and your ad platform APIs. When you ask ChatGPT to pause a campaign, it calls the Meta API directly and pauses it. Not a simulation. Not a suggestion. Actual execution.

"What if I don't use ChatGPT? I'm a Claude user."

Perfect — Claude works identically. So does Cursor IDE if you're technical. Any AI tool that supports the MCP standard connects to the same layer. You use whatever you're already comfortable with.

"Is it safe to let AI execute changes on my ad accounts?"

You control what permissions you grant. You can set it to read-only (AI advises, you approve) or full execution (AI acts immediately). Most clients start with approval mode and switch to full execution once they've seen it work. You can revoke access in 30 seconds at any time.

"We already have an agency. Why would we switch?"

Many of our clients don't switch — they keep their agency for creative strategy and use our layer for execution and reporting. The question is: does your agency execute changes in seconds via API, or in 48 hours via a human logging in? One of those costs you money every day it's not happening.

"What's the setup process? How long does it take?"

You grant standard partner access to each platform — takes about 10 minutes total. We handle all the technical setup. Within 24–48 hours your AI has full access to all connected platforms. We send you a step-by-step guide and a walkthrough video. No IT department needed.
